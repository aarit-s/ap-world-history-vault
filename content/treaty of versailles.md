[[1900-present]] / [[unit 7]]
1919, paris peace conference -- ended [[wwi]] but planted the seeds of [[wwii]]

"BIG FOUR" leaders:
	woodrow wilson (us)
	georges clemenceau (france) -- wanted germany crushed
	david lloyd george (uk)
	vittorio orlando (italy)
30 country reps total but the LOSERS were not invited:
	germany, [[ottoman]], austria-hungary, bulgaria had NO say
	russia also not invited (bolshevik gov, see [[russian revolution]])

the GOOD parts:
	created several new states in [[europe]] (czechoslovakia, yugoslavia, poland reborn)
	"mandate" system in non-european colonies (replaced direct ownership)
	established wilson's dream: LEAGUE OF NATIONS (precursor to UN)
	14 points -- wilson's idealist principles (self-determination, free trade, open diplomacy)

the BAD parts (this is what students need to know for AP):
	WAR GUILT CLAUSE (article 231) -- forced germany to accept FULL responsibility for starting the war
		caused deep national humiliation
	REPARATIONS -- germany forced to pay massive sums
		~$33 billion (massive at the time)
		caused economic devastation, hyperinflation (1923 wheelbarrows of cash for bread)
		weakened weimar republic
	MILITARY RESTRICTIONS:
		army capped at 100,000 men
		no submarines, no air force
		rhineland demilitarized
	TERRITORIAL LOSSES:
		germany lost 13% of european territory
		alsace-lorraine to france, polish corridor to poland
		all overseas colonies redistributed as league of nations mandates
	GEOPOLITICAL CHANGES:
		dismantled austro-hungarian empire -> czechoslovakia, yugoslavia (both with ethnic tensions that explode later)
		[[ottoman]] empire dismantled -> [[mustafa kemal]] creates modern turkey + british/french mandates in middle east
	SELF-DETERMINATION not fulfilled for non-european peoples (mandate system = continued [[imperialism]])
	US FAILS TO JOIN league of nations -- senate rejected the treaty
		left league with no real teeth -- couldn't enforce anything
		direct factor in why [[fascism]] could rise unchecked

how it leads to [[wwii]]:
	german resentment becomes political fuel for hitler + nazi party (see [[fascism]])
	hitler campaigns on tearing up the treaty
	economic devastation + great depression = perfect conditions for extremism
	weak league cant stop hitler when he rearms, takes austria, takes czechoslovakia, invades poland
	this is THE textbook example of how a peace treaty can cause the next war

big picture:
	intended to prevent future war -> directly caused [[wwii]]
	keynes (later [[interwar period]]) called it "a carthaginian peace" that would destabilize europe -- he was right
	classic AP DBQ topic
