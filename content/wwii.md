[[1900-present]] / [[unit 7]]
1939-1945
deadliest conflict in human history -- ~70-85 million dead

causes (direct chain from [[wwi]]):
	[[treaty of versailles]] humiliation of germany
	great depression destroyed economies (see [[interwar period]])
	rise of [[fascism]] -- hitler in germany, mussolini in italy, militarist [[japan]]
	weak league of nations couldnt enforce anything
	failed appeasement of hitler (munich 1938 -- "peace in our time", lol no)

axis vs allies:
	axis: germany, italy, [[japan]] (+ minor partners)
	allies: britain, france, soviet union (joined after 1941), us (joined after pearl harbor), [[china]] (already fighting japan since 1937)

european theater timeline:
	sept 1 1939 -- hitler invades poland (britain + france declare war)
	1940 -- germany conquers france in 6 weeks (blitzkrieg)
	1940-41 -- battle of britain (germany fails to invade britain)
	june 1941 -- hitler invades [[russia]] (operation barbarossa, breaks molotov-ribbentrop pact)
	1942-43 -- stalingrad turning point, soviet union starts pushing back
	june 6 1944 -- D-DAY normandy landings, allies retake france
	may 1945 -- berlin falls, hitler suicide, germany surrenders

pacific theater:
	dec 7 1941 -- [[japan]] attacks PEARL HARBOR -> us enters war
	1942 -- japan controls most of southeast asia + western pacific
	battle of midway 1942 = turning point
	island-hopping campaign -- us takes islands one by one
	aug 1945 -- atomic bombs HIROSHIMA + NAGASAKI
	sept 2 1945 -- japan surrenders

the HOLOCAUST:
	systematic murder of 6 MILLION jews by nazi germany
	plus millions of roma, disabled, gays, slavs, political prisoners, soviet POWs
	concentration camps + extermination camps (auschwitz, treblinka, sobibor, etc)
	"final solution" planned at wannsee conference 1942
	industrialized genocide -- gas chambers, mass shootings, death marches
	one of the defining moral catastrophes of human history

other atrocities:
	[[japan]] -- rape of nanjing 1937 (china), unit 731 biological warfare experiments, comfort women
	soviet -- katyn massacre, mass deportations
	allies firebomb dresden, tokyo (controversial)

aftermath:
	UNITED NATIONS founded 1945 (replaces failed league of nations)
	NUREMBERG TRIALS -- nazi leaders tried for crimes against humanity (new legal concept)
	UNIVERSAL DECLARATION OF HUMAN RIGHTS 1948
	cold war begins immediately -- us vs ussr
	[[decolonization]] accelerates -- european powers exhausted
	creation of israel 1948 (post-holocaust + balfour declaration aftermath)
	germany + japan rebuilt under us-led marshall plan + occupation

big picture:
	end of european dominance of world affairs
	us + ussr emerge as superpowers -> [[unit 8]] cold war
	nuclear age begins -- humanity now capable of self-extinction
	human rights as global concept
	template of "never again" (which has been violated many times since)
