[[1750-1900]]
1899-1901, [[qing]] [[china]]
anti-foreign, anti-christian uprising

who were the boxers:
	"society of righteous and harmonious fists" -- chinese martial arts group
	believed they were spiritually invincible to bullets (they were not)
	hated foreign missionaries, foreign goods, christian converts

what happened:
	attacked foreigners and chinese christians
	besieged foreign embassies in beijing for 55 days
	empress dowager cixi initially backed them

response:
	8-nation alliance (us, uk, france, germany, russia, italy, austria-hungary, japan)
	crushed the rebellion
	looted beijing
	forced china to pay massive indemnity ("boxer protocol")

why it matters:
	another failed indigenous response to [[imperialism]]
	showed how powerless qing was -- couldnt protect china or kick out foreigners
	accelerated qing collapse (fell 10 years later, 1911)
	pairs with [[sepoy mutiny]] as the two big rebellions of [[unit 6]]
