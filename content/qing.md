[[1450-1750]]
[[china]] dynasty after the ming
manchus (ethnic minority from manchuria) conquered ming china in 1644
classic [[gunpowder empires]] situation -- ethnic minority ruling huge majority

how they legitimized:
	kept the confucian buerocracy + civil service exam (didnt break what works)
	kangxi and qianlong emperors -- patrons of art and scholarship
	emperors called themselves "son of heaven" (chinese tradition)
	kangxi tolerated jesuit missionaries (for a bit)

how they kept manchu identity separate:
	queue hairstyle FORCED on han chinese men (shaved front, braid in back)
	manchus banned from intermarrying with han
	manchu language kept for official docs

expansion:
	took taiwan, mongolia, tibet, xinjiang
	one of the largest [[china]] empires ever territorially

decline (sets up unit 6):
	corruption + population pressure
	[[opium wars]] expose them as weak
	[[self strengthening]] tries to fix it but too little too late
	finally falls 1911
