[[1450-1750]]
classic [[syncretism]] -- combination of [[hinduism]] + [[islam]]
founded by guru nanak in punjab region of [[india]] around 1500
core beliefs:
	one universal god (monotheistic, like islam)
	rejected the caste system (radical break from hinduism)
	all people equal regardless of religion or status
	emphasized meditation + good works
10 gurus total -- last one declared the holy book (guru granth sahib) the final guru
golden temple in amritsar = holiest site
[[mughal]] eventually persecuted sikhs (under aurangzeb) -> sikhs militarized
became a strong regional power in punjab by 1700s
