[[1900-present]] / [[unit 7]]  
also known as ATATURK ("father of the turks")  
took over as leader after [[ottoman]] empire collapsed post-[[wwi]]  
founded modern republic of turkey 1923

reforms -- aggressive WESTERNIZATION:

- separated government from religion (secular state, "laicism")
- removed islamic law -> replaced with european legal codes (swiss civil code etc)
- encouraged western clothing, BANNED the fez hat
- expanded women's rights -- could VOTE + RUN FOR OFFICE (1934, before many western countries)
- removed arabic script for turkish language -> replaced with LATIN ALPHABET (easier to read + write, boosted literacy)
- closed religious schools -> free public education system controlled by government
- gave people surnames (he picked "ataturk")

big picture:

- classic case of defensive modernization -- like [[meiji restoration]] in [[japan]]
- but unlike japan, turkey didn't go on to colonize others
- turkey today still wrestles with ataturk's secular legacy vs islamic tradition (erdogan rolling some of it back)
- one of the cleanest examples of post-colonial state-building in world history
