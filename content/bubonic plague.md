[[1200-1450]]  
the black death, ~1347-1351  
killed hella hella hella -- like 1/3 of europe, devastated middle east + china too  
spread along [[silk roads]] thanks to [[mongols]] connectivity (pax mongolica = pax plague)  
caused by yersinia pestis bacteria, spread by fleas on rats  
made [[europe]] kinda the slums for a while

consequences:

- labor shortage -> peasants could demand more
- weakened feudalism + manorialism in [[europe]]
- church lost authority (couldnt explain or stop it)
- jewish communities scapegoated + massacred
- flagellant movements
- contributed to social unrest -> peasant revolts
- long-term: arguably set up conditions for [[europe]] renaissance + later maritime expansion

middle east + china also crushed -- this isnt just a europe story
