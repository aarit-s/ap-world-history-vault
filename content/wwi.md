[[1900-present]] / [[unit 7]]  
"the great war" 1914-1918  
the moment "old europe" ends -- no more empires running everything

why they go to war (the M.A.I.N. causes):

- MILITARISM -- buildup of armies, especially germany + britain naval race
- ALLIANCES -- the alliance system means countries CAN'T step back once their buddies start fighting
  - triple entente: britain, france, russia
  - triple alliance: germany, austria-hungary, italy (italy switches later)
  - when ottoman joined alliance, allies+entente -> central powers vs allied powers
- IMPERIALISM -- competition for colonies, what happens in [[africa]] / middle east doesnt stay there
- NATIONALISM -- bismarck-style, the balkans "powder keg", romanticized idea of war
- plus countries sold their populations on a NEED to fight via massive PROPAGANDA

the trigger -- powder keg ignites:

- june 28, 1914 -- gavrilo princip (serbian) assassinates archduke franz ferdinand (next in line for austria-hungary throne)
- princip worked for serbian terrorist group "the black hand"
- austria-hungary wants to punish serbia
- russia backs serbia, germany backs austria-hungary
- alliances kick in -> world war

germany's strategy -- the SCHLIEFFEN PLAN:

- attack france by going through belgium for quick victory
- then move army back east to fight russia
- germany's invasion of belgium = point of no return for britain
- britain HAD to fight (had treaty with belgium)

new weapons (mass destruction at unprecedented scale):

- better rifles, machine guns, mortars, flamethrowers
- zeppelins (airships)
- poison gas (chlorine, mustard)
- 1915-16: british develop the TANK
- submarines / u-boats

new style of war: TRENCH WARFARE

- western front becomes massive ditch system with deadly "no man's land" between
- battles slow + defensive -> longer + bloodier
- example: battle of verdun lasted ~11 months, ~1 million casualties french + german
- soldiers got "trench foot", terrible sanitation, shell shock (early ptsd)

really a global war:

- europe (western + eastern fronts)
- dardanelles -- british + new zealand attempt failed, ~250,000 lives lost
- middle east -- "lawrence of arabia" with arab revolt vs ottomans
- [[africa]], asia, atlantic ocean
- conscription (draft) used everywhere -- young men reached age, required to fight
  - early young population dies -> next generations encouraged to die seeing returning bodies
  - us last used draft in vietnam war

the us enters (1917) -- 3 german mistakes:

  1. germany sinks LUSITANIA (carrying us citizens) -- 1915
  2. germany renews unrestricted SUBMARINE WARFARE
  3. ZIMMERMANN TELEGRAM -- german plot getting mexico to fight us, intercepted
- us joins allies 1917 -- fresh troops + massive resources help win the war
- russia drops out 1918 due to [[russian revolution]]

end game:

- with us help, allies cannot be stopped
- germany + austria-hungary defeated
- nov 9, 1918 -- kaiser wilhelm ii of germany abdicates throne
- NOV 11, 1918 -- ARMISTICE ends fighting (why veterans day is nov 11)
- "old" [[europe]] is over -- no more empires
- end of us isolationism

armenian genocide (1915-1917):

- arguably 20th century's first genocide
- [[ottoman]] alleges armenians (predominantly christian) are secretly helping russia during wwi
- ottomans forcibly remove armenians from homes, march them on foot to "camps" in modern iraq
- similar to trail of tears
- armenians weren't actually helping russia -- ottomans lied
- 600,000 to 1.5 million armenians died from starvation, exposure, etc

after-effects:

- SPANISH FLU pandemic 1918-1920
  - soldiers returning home spread it globally
  - up to 50 million dead worldwide (more than the war itself)
  - labeled pandemic by 1919
- costs of wwi: 37 million soldiers dead, ~9 million civilians, $186 billion+ spent

[[treaty of versailles]] (paris peace conference 1919):

- "big four" leaders: woodrow wilson (us), georges clemenceau (france), david lloyd george (uk), vittorio orlando (italy)
- 30 countries' representatives but the LOSERS were not invited
  - germany, ottoman, austria-hungary, bulgaria had NO say
- russia also not invited (bolshevik gov)
- the GOOD parts:
  - created several new states in [[europe]]
  - "mandate" system in non-european colonies
  - established wilson's dream: LEAGUE OF NATIONS
- the BAD parts:
  - WAR GUILT CLAUSE (article 231) -- forced germany to accept FULL responsibility for starting war
  - REPARATIONS -- germany forced to pay massive sums -> economic devastation, hyperinflation, weakened weimar republic
  - military restrictions -- german army capped at 100,000 men, no submarines, no air force, rhineland demilitarized
  - territorial losses -- germany lost 13% of european territory + all overseas colonies
  - dismantled austro-hungarian + ottoman empires -> created czechoslovakia, yugoslavia (both with ethnic tension)
  - self-determination NOT fulfilled for non-european peoples -- mandate system = imperialism continued
  - us FAILS to join the league of nations -> league has no real teeth
- german resentment becomes the seedbed for [[fascism]] + nazi germany + wwii
