[[1900-present]] / [[unit 7]] -- 1917
the moment [[russia]] becomes the soviet union

trouble on the homefront (during [[wwi]]):
	1915 -- tsar nicholas ii took over as supreme commander of russian army, went to the front
	also 1915 -- gregory rasputin began controlling tsarina alexandra
		convinced her to appoint him as advisor
		fired several ministers
		rasputin = monk/healer/fortune teller
		established himself with royal family thanks to "ability" to help tsar's son (hemophiliac kid did get better)
	1916 -- enough was enough
		russians plan to kill rasputin during one of his feasts
		dec 1916: rasputin POISONED, SHOT, BEATEN, DROWNED, AND CASTRATED (multiple methods because he kept surviving)
	russia's chaos lets other countries take advantage (foreshadows hitler)

the revolution begins:
	feb/march 1917 -- general workers' strike begins in petrograd
		soon forces tsar nicholas to ABDICATE
		end of romanov dynasty
	provisional government takes over
	april 1917 -- VLADIMIR LENIN returns to russia
		begins undermining the provisional government
		slogan: "PEACE, LAND, AND BREAD"
		gained him + the BOLSHEVIKS many followers
		appealed to the poor (russia was mostly peasants)

lenin in control:
	march 1918 -- TREATY OF BREST-LITOVSK -- agreement with germany to remove russia from [[wwi]]
	march 1918 -- bolsheviks rename themselves the COMMUNIST PARTY, move capital to moscow
	june 1918 -- RUSSIAN CIVIL WAR begins
		REDS = bolsheviks (ppl who like lenin)
		WHITES = monarchists/anti-bolshevik (ppl who like the tsar)
	july 1918 -- lenin sends a message: EXECUTES the tsar + entire royal family
	1919 -- both sides have victories. the reds begin using FORCED LABOR CAMPS (precursor to gulags)
	1920 -- last white general surrenders. communists officially in charge of russia (soon to be soviet union)

lenin's economic policies:
	began MARXIST farm collectivization for peasants
		government would gather all food + distribute it evenly (pure communism)
		FAILS because: large families get less than what they produced, not enough food production
	1921 -- NEW ECONOMIC POLICY (NEP)
		controversial -- includes aspects of CAPITALISM rather than pure communism
		government controlled high-level industries + companies
		BUT farms + smaller businesses stay with the people for profit
		pushes people to do better, gets farmers to pick up slack
		modern parallel: china opening up its economy

1922 -- lenin has massive stroke and dies
soviet union needs new leader -> [[stalin]] takes over

big picture:
	first successful communist revolution in world history
	inspired communist movements globally (china, vietnam, cuba)
	creates the soviet union -> sets up cold war era
	completely transforms russia from an agricultural autocracy to industrial superpower (eventually)
