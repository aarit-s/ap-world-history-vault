[[1900-present]]  
[[cold war]] + decolonization (1945-1991)

[[cold war]] -- us (capitalism, democracy) vs ussr ([[stalin]]'s communism, authoritarian)

- never direct war between them ([[mutually assured destruction]] via nukes)
- [[marshall plan]] 1948 -- us pumps $13B into western europe to stop communism
- NATO (1949) vs WARSAW PACT (1955)
- iron curtain divides europe
- [[berlin wall]] (1961-1989) -- physical symbol of division
- [[cuban missile crisis]] 1962 -- closest the world came to nuclear war
- [[prague spring]] 1968 -- czechoslovakia tries reform, ussr crushes it
- SPACE RACE -- soviet sputnik 1957, us moon landing 1969
- arms race -- nuclear, conventional, espionage

PROXY WARS (cold war hot conflicts):

- korean war 1950-1953 -- north (communist) vs south (us-backed), ends in stalemate at 38th parallel
- [[vietnam]] war 1955-1975 -- north vietnam (ho chi minh, communist) wins, us loses
- soviet-afghan war 1979-1989 -- us backs mujahideen against soviets, soviets lose (sets up taliban + al-qaeda later)
- angola, nicaragua, congo, etc -- communist vs capitalist proxies in [[decolonization]]

[[decolonization]] -- main theme of unit 8:

- [[india]] (1947) -- [[gandhi]], partition with pakistan
- [[africa]] -- ghana 1957 (kwame nkrumah), kenya 1963 (jomo kenyatta), algeria 1962, angola 1975, etc
- [[vietnam]] from france
- philippines from us 1946
- indonesia from netherlands
- apartheid in south africa ends 1994 with mandela presidency

[[china]] -- communist revolution:

- 1949 -- mao zedong wins civil war, founds peoples republic of china
- GREAT LEAP FORWARD 1958-62 -- forced collectivization, 30+ million die in famine
- CULTURAL REVOLUTION 1966-76 -- mao purges intellectuals, students attack teachers, chaos
- mao dies 1976 -> deng xiaoping opens china to capitalism (special economic zones)
- tiananmen square 1989 -- democracy protest crushed

[[middle east modern]]:

- creation of israel 1948 (balfour declaration aftermath)
- arab-israeli wars 1948, 1967, 1973
- iranian revolution 1979 -- shah overthrown, ayatollah khomeini
- iran-iraq war 1980-88

LATIN AMERICA:

- cuban revolution 1959 -- fidel castro takes over -> communist
- us-backed coups + dictators across region (chile pinochet, argentina, etc)
- liberation theology -- catholic clergy fighting for the poor

CIVIL RIGHTS movements:

- us civil rights -- mlk, march on washington 1963, civil rights act 1964
- feminism -- 2nd wave, betty friedan, roe v wade 1973
- lgbtq rights begins -- stonewall 1969
- anti-apartheid in south africa

end of cold war:

- mikhail gorbachev (ussr) -- glasnost (openness) + perestroika (restructuring) reforms backfire
- 1989 -- berlin wall falls, eastern european communist govts collapse one by one
- 1991 -- soviet union DISSOLVES into 15 separate countries
- us = lone superpower -> sets up [[unit 9]]

big themes:

- bipolar world (us vs ussr) -> unipolar (us)
- end of european empires
- rise of mass media + global culture
- beginning of nuclear age
- human rights becomes global concept (un universal declaration 1948)
