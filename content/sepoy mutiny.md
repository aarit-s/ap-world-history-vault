[[1750-1900]]  
also called the indian rebellion of 1857 (use this name on the AP -- "mutiny" is the british framing)

setup:

- british east india company controlled most of [[india]]
- used sepoys -- indian soldiers under british officers
- growing resentment: cultural disrespect, economic exploitation, missionary pressure

trigger:

- new rifle cartridges greased with cow + pig fat
- cow = sacred to hindus
- pig = haram for muslims
- soldiers had to bite the cartridge open
- BOTH religions offended at once -- hindu and muslim sepoys united

what happened:

- mutiny spread across north india
- delhi, kanpur, lucknow major battles
- british put it down brutally with reinforcements
- massacres on both sides

consequences (HUGE):

- british east india company DISSOLVED
- india now under direct british crown rule = the british raj
- queen victoria became "empress of india" 1877
- end of mughal dynasty (last emperor exiled)
- beginning of organized indian nationalism -> indian national congress 1885

big picture:

- classic indigenous response to [[imperialism]] -- failed militarily but seeded the independence movement
