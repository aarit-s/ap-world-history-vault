[[1900-present]] / [[interwar period]] / [[unit 7]]  
joseph stalin -- took over soviet union after lenin's stroke + death (1924)  
real name iosif dzhugashvili (georgian)  
ruler of [[russia]] / soviet union 1924-1953

economic policies -- the 5-YEAR PLANS:

- all people had 5 years to make everything they could for the state
- this is a COMMAND ECONOMY (state controls everything)
- rapid industrialization at huge human cost
- collectivization of agriculture -- farms taken from peasants, made state-owned
- created FAMINES on the farms
  - ukraine HOLODOMOR 1932-33 -- millions starved (some argue genocide)
  - thousands more starved across soviet union

tools of terror (related to machiavelli):

- GULAG SYSTEM -- siberian forced labor camps
  - sent millions of "enemies of the state" there
- SECRET POLICE (NKVD, later KGB)
- GREAT PURGE 1936-38 -- executed/jailed military officers, party members, intellectuals
  - wiped out anyone who could possibly threaten him

cult of personality:

- first ruler to truly create a CULT OF PERSONALITY
- stalin pictures + statues everywhere
- became god-like figure all should worship
- censorship + propaganda everywhere
- "stalin everywhere"

paradox: stalin was a dictator BUT soviet union was supposed to be communist (all equal)

- clearly not equal
- this contradiction underlies all 20th century communism

WWII role:

- 1939 -- molotov-ribbentrop pact with hitler (non-aggression treaty)
  - secretly divides eastern europe
- 1941 -- hitler invades anyway (operation barbarossa)
- soviet union joins allies, suffers MASSIVE casualties (~27 million dead, more than any country)
- red army takes berlin 1945

post-wwii:

- occupies eastern europe -> creates communist satellite states
- sets up COLD WAR with us
- develops atomic bomb 1949
- dies 1953 -> khrushchev eventually denounces him in "secret speech" 1956

big picture:

- transformed soviet union from agricultural backwater to industrial superpower
- but at cost of 20+ million dead from purges, gulags, famines
- cold war shaped by his decisions
- created template for 20th century totalitarianism alongside [[fascism]]
