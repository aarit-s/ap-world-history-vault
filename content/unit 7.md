[[1900-present]]
global conflict (1900-1945)
two world wars + interwar period

[[wwi]] (1914-1918):
	M.A.I.N. causes: militarism, alliances, imperialism, nationalism
	trigger: gavrilo princip assassinates archduke franz ferdinand june 28 1914
	trench warfare, machine guns, poison gas, tanks, submarines
	first global war
	armenian genocide 1915-17 (ottoman vs armenian christians)
	us joins 1917 (lusitania, u-boats, zimmermann telegram)
	russia drops out 1918 ([[russian revolution]])
	germany surrenders nov 11 1918
	[[treaty of versailles]] (in [[wwi]] note) -- punishes germany, league of nations, sets up wwii

[[russian revolution]] 1917:
	tsar nicholas ii abdicates after wwi disasters
	lenin + bolsheviks take over -> soviet union
	russian civil war (reds vs whites) -> communists win
	[[stalin]] eventually takes over

[[interwar period]] (1918-1939):
	great depression 1929 -- global economic catastrophe
	[[fascism]] rises -- mussolini in italy, hitler in germany
	[[stalin]] in ussr -- 5 year plans, gulags, purges
	[[gandhi]] + indian independence movement (civil disobedience, salt march)
	[[mustafa kemal]] creates modern turkey
	[[middle east modern]] -- balfour declaration, pan-arabism
	cultural changes -- harlem renaissance, cubism, surrealism, women's liberation
	keynesian economics + new deal

WWII (1939-1945):
	hitler invades poland sept 1939
	axis: germany, italy, japan
	allies: britain, france, soviet union (after 1941), us (after pearl harbor dec 7 1941)
	holocaust -- 6 million jews + millions others systematically murdered
	pacific war -- japan vs us
	atomic bombs hiroshima + nagasaki aug 1945 -> japan surrenders
	~70-85 million dead, deadliest conflict in history
	creates: united nations, decolonization push, cold war
