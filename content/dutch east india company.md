[[1450-1750]]  
VOC (vereenigde oost-indische compagnie), founded 1602  
THE prototype [[joint stock companies]] -- arguably first true multinational corporation  
dutch govt gave it monopoly on dutch trade in [[indian ocean trade]] / asia  
had its own:

- army
- navy
- ability to make treaties + wage war
- ability to coin money
basically a country pretending to be a business

dutch context:

- netherlands gained independence from spain 1581 (provincial autonomy)
- bank of amsterdam = the best bank ever, industrialized foreign + local currency
- tolerated jews (esp those expelled from spain/portugal)
- henry hudson explored for the dutch -- ended up in north america, founded NEW AMSTERDAM
  - new amsterdam later became new york when english took it

what it did:

- took over [[southeast asia]] from portuguese -- malacca, indonesia
- headquartered in batavia (modern jakarta)
- monopoly on the spice trade (nutmeg, cloves, mace from "spice islands")
- took over taiwan briefly
- traded with [[japan]] from dejima during isolation
- wiped out the population of the banda islands to control nutmeg

decline:

- corruption + competition from british east india company
- bankrupt by 1799, dissolved
- indonesia became a dutch crown colony

big picture:

- prototype for modern corporations
- prototype for [[imperialism]] via private companies (foreshadows belgium's congo, etc)
