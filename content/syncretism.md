when two cultures or religions mix and form something new  
NOT the same as one replacing the other -- both elements stay visible

[[1450-1750]] examples (CB always asks for these):

- vodun (haiti) -- west african religion + catholic saints
- santeria (cuba) -- yoruba gods mapped to catholic saints
- candomble (brazil) -- same idea
- virgin of guadalupe (mexico) -- catholic mary appearing as an indigenous woman
- [[sikhism]] -- [[hinduism]] + [[islam]] in india
- mestizo / mulatto / casta system -- racial syncretism in spanish americas
- [[mughal]] empire tolerated religions lead to [[sikhism]]

earlier examples:

- [[buddhism]] in [[china]] mixed with daoism -> chan/zen
- angkor wat -- started hindu, became buddhist (see [[southeast asia]])
- swahili culture -- bantu + arab in east [[africa]]

why it happens:

- conquest doesnt fully erase a culture
- missionaries adapt to convert people more easily
- enslaved + indigenous people preserve their religion under a catholic mask
