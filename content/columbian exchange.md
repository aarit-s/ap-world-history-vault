[[1450-1750]]
the transfer of diseases, food, plants, animals, and people between the eastern (afro-eurasia) and western ([[americas]]) hemispheres after 1492

DISEASE (catastrophic for [[americas]]):
	smallpox, measles, influenza, typhus, malaria
	indigenous americans had no immunity
	~90% of indigenous population died in some regions within a century
	this is WHY european conquest was even possible
	this is WHY [[atlantic slave trade]] happened (no labor force left)

FOOD from americas -> rest of world (the W column for everyone else):
	potatoes -- huge for [[europe]] (esp ireland, [[russia]]) and led to population boom -> set up [[industrial revolution]]
	corn (maize) -- huge in africa + china
	tomatoes -- italian food doesnt exist without this
	cacao (chocolate)
	tobacco
	chili peppers -- now in indian + thai food, etc
	cassava -- staple in africa

FOOD from old world -> americas:
	wheat, rice, sugar, bananas, coffee, citrus
	sugar especially became a plantation crop driving slavery

ANIMALS old -> new:
	horses (changed plains native american life entirely)
	cattle, pigs, sheep, chickens

ANIMALS new -> old:
	turkeys (less impactful)

PEOPLE:
	europeans + africans (mostly forced) -> americas
	syphilis maybe went the other way (debated)

CULTURAL:
	[[syncretism]] (vodun, santeria, virgin of guadalupe)
	creole languages
	the casta system

big picture: created the first truly global ecological + economic system
