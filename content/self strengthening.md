[[1750-1900]]
[[qing]] [[china]] reform movement, 1860s-1890s, after [[opium wars]] losses

slogan: "chinese learning for the essence, western learning for practical use"
	keep confucian values + government structure
	just adopt western tech (guns, ships, factories)

what they did:
	built modern arsenals + shipyards
	opened a foreign language school
	sent students abroad
	tried to build a modern navy

why it failed:
	conservatives at court (esp empress dowager cixi) blocked deeper reform
	cant just bolt on western tech without western institutions
	stole from the navy budget to build a marble boat at the summer palace (yes really)
	exposed by sino-japanese war (1894-95) -- japan, which DID full reform via [[meiji restoration]], demolished china
	then [[boxer rebellion]], then qing falls 1911

big picture:
	the FAILURE case in the defensive modernization trio
	[[meiji restoration]] = success
	[[tanzimat]] = mid
	self-strengthening = L
	lesson: half-measures dont work when the other guys are full industrial
