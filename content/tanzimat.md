[[1750-1900]]
[[ottoman]] reform movement, 1839-1876
"tanzimat" = "reorganization"

why:
	ottoman empire = "sick man of europe"
	losing land + influence to russia, britain, france
	needed to modernize or die

what they did:
	westernized the military (european-style training + weapons)
	new legal code based on french law
	equality for non-muslims (christians, jews) on paper
	modern schools, postal service, telegraph
	first ottoman constitution 1876

results:
	mixed -- some modernization, but conservatives (sultans, religious scholars) pushed back
	young turks movement (1908) eventually overthrew the sultan
	empire still collapsed after wwi, BUT modernization seeds led to modern turkey

big picture:
	one of the "big three" defensive modernization responses with [[self strengthening]] (china) and [[meiji restoration]] (japan)
	tanzimat = mid-success
	self-strengthening = failure
	meiji = full success
	CB pairs these constantly
