[[1200-1450]] / [[unit 1]]  
christian state in the horn of [[africa]] -- huge outlier in a region dominated by [[islam]] + indigenous beliefs

religion:

- ETHIOPIAN ORTHODOX TEWAHEDO CHURCH (its own thing, split from coptic christianity later)
- unique canon -- the KEBRA NAGAST ("glory of the kings") is their core text
- this is the religion that legitimizes the whole state
- see [[christianity]]

political structure:

- emperor = NEGUS NEGAST ("king of kings")
- claimed direct descent from KING SOLOMON + the QUEEN OF SHEBA -- divine legitimacy plug
- nobility ranks: RAS + DEJAZMACH -- got land in exchange for military service (parallel to feudal [[europe]])
- clergy = HUGE landowners + ran education + legitimized the throne
- classic religion-as-state-building

economy:

- long distance trade -- gold, ivory, incense
- connected to mediterranean (venetian merchants) + arabian ports (aden)
- NOT cut off -- ethiopia is plugged into [[indian ocean trade]] adjacent networks

contrast with great zimbabwe:

- zimbabwe = indigenous shamanism, no scripture, regional trade
- ethiopia = christian, scripture, diplomatic ties with byzantine + later european christian powers
- ethiopia's religion shapes its FOREIGN policy -- it can talk to europe as a fellow christian state

other ethiopia hits:

- LALIBELA rock churches -- carved straight DOWN into bedrock, 12th-13th century
- stays independent forever -- beats italy at adwa 1896 (one of two african states never colonized in [[scramble for africa]])
- mussolini finally invades 1935-41 (revenge for adwa) but italians get kicked out

big picture:

- classic AP comparison: ethiopia (christian state) vs mali (muslim state) vs great zimbabwe (indigenous state) -- all building states in africa same era using DIFFERENT ideologies for legitimacy
- proves religion ≠ destiny in africa -- the continent had every major religion as a state-builder
